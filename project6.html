WEATHER FORECAST APP

The Weather Forecast App provides real-time temperature updates for any location you enter. Simply input the name of a city or region, and the app will instantly fetch and display the current temperature for that area. This intuitive and user-friendly app helps you stay informed about the weather conditions wherever you are, making it easier to plan your day and dress accordingly. Whether you're traveling, commuting, or just curious about the weather, this app ensures you have up-to-date temperature information at your fingertips.
