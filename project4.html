ALPHABET-LEARNING APP

Welcome to the Alphabet Learning App, a fun and interactive way for kids to learn the alphabet! Upon opening the app, you'll be greeted with cheerful music designed to captivate young learners. After the welcome music fades out, you'll be ready to explore the alphabet. Click on any letter, and an image will appear below, accompanied by a tutorial on how to pronounce that letter correctly. This engaging approach combines visual and auditory learning, making it easy for children to grasp and remember each letter.
