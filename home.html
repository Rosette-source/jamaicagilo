This website is a project for my IT ELEC 3 instructor, Dean Dr. Valen Alzate,
at Isabela State University. Initially, I thought only she would see my website.
However, she informed us that our websites would be accessible on the browser,
allowing anyone interested to visit.

When I started this project, I was unsure how to begin.
I didn't know what the website should look like or what content to include to
truly represent myself as a second-year BSIT student. My goal was to create a
site that is simple yet meaningful, useful, relevant, and current.
I wanted it to be a resource that fellow aspiring writers, artists, servants,
and designers would find valuable and inspiring.

This website features several sections.
In the ABOUT section, I share insights about my personal interests,
things I don't usually prefer and things I'm good and bad at.
MY JOURNEY section contains a short timeline how I started from my college life,
and how I am able to make those projects under my PROJECTS section.
In addition to that, the PROJECTS section showcases snippets and highlights from
my IT ELEC 3 coursework. I also included some memorable images in the GALLERY
section to give you guys ideas about me. While the BLOG section
includes essays answering the top 5 frequent "WHYs" people asked me about.
And finally, there's a CONTACT section where
you can find my social media accounts to connect with me.

I am committed to regularly updating this website to ensure
that the content remains current and continues to reflect my
growth and experiences. I hope you find this website both insightful
and enjoyable. Thank you for taking the time to visit!
