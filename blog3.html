WHY "MALEFICENT" IS MY PEN NAME

I still remember the day a friend of mine saw a sketch I had made for her. She was amazed by the drawing, but her surprise turned into curiosity when she noticed the name "Maleficent" written below it. She actually doubted whether I was the artist or if I had borrowed the sketch from somewhere else.

Choosing "Maleficent" as my pen name was deeply personal and tied to a challenging period in my life. After my first break-up, I found solace in sketching and used art as an outlet to express my feelings. During that time, I chose the name "Maleficent" for my sketches because I felt a connection to the character.

Maleficent, with her story of betrayal and reclaiming power, resonated with me. I saw her as a reflection of my own journey through heartbreak and healing. Using her name was like a way of embracing my emotions and turning them into something meaningful. Although I don't use "Maleficent" as my pen name anymore it was an important part of my artistic journey. It helped me channel my pain into creativity and provided a way to express my emotions during a tough time.

Looking back, I'm grateful for the inspiration it gave me and the personal growth that came with it.
